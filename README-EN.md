## 🧑‍💻 About Me

Developer passionate about **low-level systems**, **cybersecurity**, and **high-performance web experiences**. Currently, my main focus is the convergence between **Rust, WebAssembly**, and **systems architecture**, exploring how to bring true computing to the browser.

- 🚀 **Leading** development of experimental ecosystems (TRYMON-OS, R.A.M 3D, 33XL Games)
- 🛡️ **Specialist** in Elicitation and Computational Forensics applied to CTFs and Security
- ⚙️ **Exploring** Rust, WASM, Three.js, and Operating Systems Architecture
- 🎮 **Creating** immersive and metaphysical experiences in digital media
- 🤝 **Open** to partnerships in open-source projects, Game Engines, and Cybersecurity Research

> **Note**: I'm also an artist and creative author under the brand **[@TRYPER-33](https://github.com/TRYPER-33)**, which encompasses works like *Self Pandora*, *Whisper Valley*, and creative universes distributed by **33XL Games System**. This account reflects my identity as a **developer and systems engineer**.
